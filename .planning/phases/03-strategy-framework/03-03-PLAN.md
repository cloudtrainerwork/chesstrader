**Volatility Strategy Requirements:**
- **Long Straddle**: Long call + Long put (same strike, same expiration) - profits from large moves either direction
- **Short Straddle**: Short call + Short put (same strike, same expiration) - profits from low volatility/sideways movement
- **Long Strangle**: Long call + Long put (different strikes, same expiration) - cheaper than straddle, wider breakeven
- **Short Strangle**: Short call + Short put (different strikes, same expiration) - credit strategy, profits from range-bound movement

**Volatility Regime Integration:**
- Long volatility strategies: Entry in Low Volatility (regime 4) before volatility expansion
- Short volatility strategies: Entry in High Volatility (regime 3) expecting volatility contraction
- Exit triggers: Volatility regime transitions, profit targets, loss limits
- Crisis regime (8): Special handling due to extreme volatility conditions
